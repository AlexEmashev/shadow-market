How To

Description

This article describes how to use Shadow Market app.

How to buy item

You don't buy an item directly from the app, rather have contact to seller so you can discuss final price and shipment options.

  • Go to catalog
  • Chose item you like
  • If you want to accuire an item right away, you can click "Buy" button
  • In other case you can click "Details" to see item detailed description
  • Clicking "Buy" button brings the seller contact dialog
  • Contact the seller to negotiate a deal

How to sell item

To sell the item from Shadow Market follow the steps below:

  • First you need to login to the app. For demo purposes, use existing user names mentioned on items without a password
  • Go to My Items section
  • Click "New Item" button
  • Set title, description, price and don't forget about photos
  • Click "Save" now you can see your item in the catalog so do other visitors
  • You can always update your item from My Items chosing you item
  • Don't forget to delete item as soon as you sell it. In My Items menu chose item to remove and use floating menu to the right
